CASH RAID — Game Design & Implementation Document
Project Overview

Cash Raid is a fast-paced multiplayer team shooter inspired by classic Capture the Flag gameplay, but instead of stealing flags, players steal MONEY from enemy vaults and use it to buy weapons, upgrades, and defenses during the match.

The game focuses on:

fast movement
team coordination
risk vs reward
economy management
high-action combat

The base multiplayer shooter already exists.
This document focuses only on implementing the new gameplay systems.

CORE GAMEPLAY
Main Objective

Two teams compete to steal money from the enemy vault and safely return it to their own base.

The team with the highest banked money at the end of the match wins.

Alternative win condition:

First team to reach target money wins instantly.

Example:

Match Duration: 15 minutes
Win Target: $100,000
CORE GAME LOOP
Spawn at team base
Attack enemy players or enemy vault
Collect money
Carry money back safely
Deposit money into team vault
Use money to buy upgrades/weapons
Repeat until timer ends
MONEY SYSTEM
Types of Money
1. Carried Money

Money currently held by a player.

Rules:

visible on HUD
dropped on death
can be stolen by anyone
2. Banked Money

Money safely deposited into team vault.

Rules:

cannot be lost
contributes to win condition
shared team resource
PLAYER DEATH RULES

When a player dies:

drop 70% of carried money
remaining 30% disappears
dropped money remains on ground for 30 seconds
both teams can collect it

This creates:

comeback moments
ambush gameplay
intense team fights
TEAM VAULT SYSTEM

Each team has a protected vault area.

Players must:

enter vault zone
interact for 2 seconds
deposit carried money

After deposit:

money added to team total
player wallet resets to 0
BUY SYSTEM

Players can spend money during the match.

Buy Stations

Located inside team base.

Players can purchase:

Optional future upgrades:

PLAYER RISK SYSTEM

The more money a player carries:

the more visible they become
slight movement speed reduction
optional glowing effect on minimap

Purpose:

increase tension
encourage teamwork
create “protect the carrier” moments
MOST WANTED SYSTEM

If a player carries a very high amount of money:

marked as “MOST WANTED”
periodically revealed on minimap
enemy team receives bounty objective

Example threshold:

carrying over $15,000

Reward:

bonus cash for killing Most Wanted player

FINAL GAME SUMMARY

Cash Raid is a team-based multiplayer shooter where players raid enemy vaults, steal money, defend their own bank, and use cash to upgrade their power during the match.

Every fight impacts the economy.

Every death can change the game.

The goal is to combine:

arena shooter speed
capture-the-flag strategy
extraction-style risk
real-time economy progression

into one highly replayable competitive experience.
